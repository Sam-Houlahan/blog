What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important.

Post by Sam Houlahan, March 07, 2017.

What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important?

A responsive site is when you develop your website to change around in order to suit the screensize you are viewing it on. This is a very important practice with most people in this age having multiple devices.
It is essential your website looks amazing on any screensize.

What mobile first design is, and why it's important?

This is when you develop your website taking in mobile considerations first as opposed to desktop. This is becoming a more popular way for web development as so many users are viewing websites on their phones.

What frameworks are, and their pros and cons?

A framework is a software designed to support the deveopment of websites or web applications. Pro's would be that using a framework will save you time by automating common activities performed in web development. The cons would be that your website or web application may end up looking or functioning similar to someone else's project that is using the same framework.

What a wireframe is and why we use it?

A wireframe in web development is similar to a blueprint and is an important step in any design process. Before you start developing it is essential that you and your client are on the same page, wireframing plays a big role in this as it is a mock up of what the finished product will look like.

The aspects of your wireframes you found difficult to implement, and why.
