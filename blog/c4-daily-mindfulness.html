Emotional Intelligence
Pt 3

Post by Sam Houlahan, March 19, 2017.

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learned that this is a valuable concept as for me personally I find I do sometimes get distracted in conversations and have to ask them to repeat. Where as when being mindful using the listening,looping,dipping methods it is incredible how much more you can actually take from a simple conversation. The person I tried it on was suprised when I told them I was learning Mindful Meditation, however they went along with it and said they felt it was nice and could tell they had my full attention in the conversation.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I feel I will use the "Do less than you can" method as I have been trying to commit to 10 minutes when meditating. However once I start to get to around the 7 minute mark it starts to feel a little bit like a chore. Reading about this method it is nice to know that this is a common thing to occur when learning, so I will start stopping once I get this feeling.

Any other musings?

I found the walking meditation concept interesting and I am looking forward to trying it on my next walk through a nice peaceful enviroment!.
