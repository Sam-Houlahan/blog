Emotional Intelligence
Pt 4

Post by Sam Houlahan, March 22, 2017.

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness is what gives us an understanding of what we are doing, our strengths and weaknesses, our likes and dislikes. The relationship with mindfullness would be how you can give observation to the self awareness without judgement.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried the Body Scan practice. I felt it was very interesting all though a lot of the body parts I was scanning I couldn't guage much feeling or sensations, however I could feel strong sensations and feelings in my hands and feet while in this state, I assume the other parts will come with more practice. I felt good afterwards like I always do after meditating.

A What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

It was interesting especially the end metaphor. It is very true that emotions and thoughts aren't what or who we are they simply come an go. With this in mind I feel it will become easier to master emotions, while keeping in mind that you are just experiencing an emotion and that is not what you are.
