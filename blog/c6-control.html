Control

Post by Sam Houlahan, 02 April, 2017.

When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

When I am approached with problems/challenges that I dont know how to solve I feel alot of bad emotions. This particuarly happend alot this week on the Minesweeper challenges, I found this week was the hardest for me so far. I felt alot of self doubt about how I will handle bootcamp when I am struggling in week 6 of phase 0. However knowing I have support from EDA teachers and my cohort that I can turn to when I need is a good feeling. It has made me realise how important mindfulness is for situaitons like this when I am overcome with bad emotions.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I felt it was a good excercise. I will be using this excercise for sure in the challenges in the weeks to come and I wish I read this chapter sooner so I could've used it in the challenges I faced earlier in the week.
